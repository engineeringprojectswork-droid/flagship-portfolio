## The story (true): leads under a dollar

A measured 2026 Meta pilot for a **B2B industrial supplier** in Kuwait (misting fans,
access equipment, scaffolding). Buyers there don't fill forms — they open WhatsApp. He
ran click-to-WhatsApp ads and turned **$172.78 of spend into 206 WhatsApp conversations
— about $0.84 each** (best campaign $0.50).

**Honesty (keep):** these are *leads/conversations*, not closed revenue — he generates
the leads, the company's engineer prices and closes.
