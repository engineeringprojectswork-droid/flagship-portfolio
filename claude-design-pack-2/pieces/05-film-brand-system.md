## The story (true): one person, the output of a studio

For a misting-products line (**"Niagara Fog"**) Mohamed built a **complete bilingual
brand system** — not just posts, but the *rules everything is made from*: design tokens &
UI kits, 18 HTML preview pages, and a 10-page A4 print catalog. Across the wider content
work he produced **~254 production files, including ~144 videos** — using Canva, After
Effects and Premiere; AI video (Kling, Wan) on rented GPUs; and AI restoration of hundreds
of legacy photos. One person, the output of a studio.

**Honesty (keep):** the file/video counts come from the company's production archive.
