## The story (true): 0 → 1M+ followers

For ~4 years (2021–2025) Mohamed owned the entire social presence of **Al-Ma'ali, an
Islamic satellite channel** — strategy, content calendar, per-platform optimisation —
turning broadcast clips into millions of views a month. He grew **Facebook from 9.2K to
over a million** followers, and built three more platforms from near zero.

**Honesty (keep):** these are public-channel **follower counts**, self-reported from the
channels he ran; a million Facebook followers is publicly verifiable. The project
concluded in 2025.
