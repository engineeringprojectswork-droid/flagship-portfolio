The problem

Leads were arriving. Nothing was holding them.

Ads drove WhatsApp chats, but there was no shared place to track status, priority, who owns what, and which spend produced which lead. So I designed the system the company didn’t have.

What I built

A whole pipeline, in a spreadsheet.

01

Status taxonomy & color-coding

Every lead has a status, a priority, and a conditional color so the pipeline reads at a glance.

02

Dashboard & ad-spend → lead funnel

A tab that links Meta ad spend to the leads it produced — closing the loop between marketing and money.

03

Engineer hand-off queue

Qualified leads route cleanly to the engineer who prices and closes.

04

Accountability / SLA tab

Owner-by-status, a day-counter and fault scoring that surfaced stuck-lead bottlenecks.
